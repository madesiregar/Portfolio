About
Shaping Skills
Across Six Semesters

I am a sixth-semester Cyber Security student at Polibatam University, Batam. My academic journey has taken me through a wide range of security disciplines — from classical cryptography to real-world SIEM deployments in a full SOC environment.

I am drawn to both the offensive and defensive sides of security. Understanding how attacks are engineered is what sharpens my ability to defend against them. Each project I take on brings me one step closer to becoming a well-rounded security professional.

Beyond the technical, I believe security is also about communication — being able to articulate threats, write clearly, and educate others. That conviction led me to publish an article on digital security awareness for a wider public audience.
